Ministries

The Ministries

Main Ministry
11:00 am every Sunday

Our heart is to see lives transformed by the love of Christ and to GLORIFY HIM in all we do. You'll be greeted with warm smiles, hear uplifting and worshipful music to encourage you and powerful preaching from God’s word. God didn’t just save you from something, He saved you for something! Discovering your kingdom purpose is a journey and we’re committed to help you every step of the way.

Sunday School (Kid's Ministry)
10:30 am every Sunday.

LightHouse Temple is a place where kids feel God’s ridiculous love while having crazy fun. They learn about Jesus in age-appropriate spaces and a safe, secure environment. We desire to come alongside you and provide resources for your family to be healthy and equipped, and to empower every kid to be a life-long disciple of Christ. Our goal is to provide them with a teaching opportunity the moment they walk in the door.

Choir Ministry

As a choir, our goal is to glorify God at our highest possible level of excellence. Apart from leading the Worship service on Sunday, our choir often prepares other Musical events, typically at Christmas and Easter.

Youth Ministry

We foster a culture of revival and honor. We focus on connecting with God in prayer and worship, experiencing Biblical community through Groups, and living a healthy spiritual, emotional, and physical lifestyle.

Bus Ministry

The bus ministry is the single greatest evangelistic tool in the history of the world. No other ministry of the local church has been responsible for more people coming to church and coming to Christ than the bus ministry.

Support widows and Orphans

Children who are orphans are unlikely to receive any education.Donations to Lighthouse are paying the fees of many orphans in primary and secondary schools helping their lives, and those of the widows to be transformed.

Support National Pastors

Lighthouse provides supports national pastors in an effort to help establish and grow churches in Kenya which allows them to dedicate most of their time to the ministry without worrying about how they can provide for their family.
